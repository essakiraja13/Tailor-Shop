Measurement Guide

How to measure yourself perfectly at home

Blouse Measurement Guide

1. Shoulder

Measure from one shoulder bone to the other across the back.

2. Chest/Bust

Measure around the fullest part of your chest, keeping tape relaxed.

3. Waist

Measure around your natural waistline where the blouse ends.

Salwar & Kurti Guide

1. Full Length

Measure from shoulder to your desired length (knee/ankle).

2. Hip

Measure around the widest part of your hips.

3. Sleeve Length

Measure from shoulder edge to desired sleeve end.

Men's Shirt & Pant

Follow these simple steps for a sharp fit.

Shirt: Collar

Measure around the neck, leaving one finger gap.

Shirt: Chest

Measure under armpits around the fullest part of chest.

Pant: Waist

Measure where you normally wear your pants.
